]]> This analysis screens your oil sample for presence and amount of all estradiol esters listed in PARAMETERS section below.

With blind testing, you do not need to tell us which of the estradiol esters you suspect in your sample. All on the list will get detected and their amount measured, even in mixtures.

WARNING: Estradiol hemihydrate will be identified as estradiol no ester (estradiol base) due to limitations of the method.
