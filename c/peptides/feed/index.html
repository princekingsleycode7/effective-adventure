]]> ID, amount and purity analysis of Dihexa.

You can find the test details in the parameters section below.

Disclaimer:

In case of lyophilizated dihexa sample we can dissolve it in:

1. H2O, which would be also used in human consumption of lyophilizated dihexa. However, dihexa is not soluble in water and therefore it would most likely get filtered out during the testing. Only soluble samples can be analyzed and insoluble particles are filtered out.

2. DMSO, which would dissolve dihexa and therefore nothing would get filtered out during the analysis. However, you cannot inject DMSO into a person therefore it would not correspond with safe use of the sample.

3. Methanol, which is the best for Dihexa dissolution. However, same as DMSO, it would make it uninjectable into body in case of human consumption.

Option no.1 is the default testing procedure, in case you want to go with option no. 2 or no. 3 please choose the solvent from additional services.

In case your sample is tablet or capsule, we recommend the default option.
