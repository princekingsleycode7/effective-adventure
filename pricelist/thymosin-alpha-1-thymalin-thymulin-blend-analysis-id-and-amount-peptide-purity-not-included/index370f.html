Can I expedite my testing?
Yes, you can! Please write us a number of the order you want to rush and choose “Expedited testing” as subject. You can either rush the whole order, or specific tests. What are additional services?
Additional services present you with enhanced harm reduction tests and test specific options for the analysis of your sample. Please mind in case you select any additional service for the sample, our e-shop needs to put them in the cart together with the said sample. When you have more samples of the same test with additional services, you need to add the samples to your cart one by one. For example: you want to test Tirzepatide 15 mg with sterility testing and Tirzepatide 30 mg with heavy metals testing, you need to add the sample one by one.
5
Do I need to send more pieces of sample for additional services?
It’s recommended for some of them, sending less may impact the results. Let’s tackle it one by one: GCMS and LCMS - no additional piece of sample is necessary; Heavy metals analysis - 1 tab/caps, 1 ml of solution, 200 mg raw powder, 1 hole vial of lyophilizate; Sterility testing - 2x whole unopened peptide vial, 2x unopened 1ml ampoule,1x unopened 10ml vial, unopened pieces are needed as to prevent contamination before testing; Endotoxin analysis - 1 vial (2 recommended) or 1 ml (2ml recommended), CHNS mass report - no additional piece of sample is necessary; Iodide/Chloride analysis - 1 tablet/capsule, PH measurement - no additional piece of sample is necessary; Fentanyl presence analysis - no additional piece of sample is necessary, TFA analysis - no additional piece of sample is necessary.
