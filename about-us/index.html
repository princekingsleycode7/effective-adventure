About Janoshik

Pioneering Laboratory Analysis

At Janoshik, we specialize in cutting-edge laboratory analysis of anabolic compounds, peptides, SARMs, and more. Our commitment to scientific excellence, confidentiality, and transparency has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Who We Are

Founded with the mission of ensuring product quality and consumer safety, Janoshik is an independent testing laboratory offering reliable and verifiable analytical services. We collaborate with researchers, athletes, and industry professionals to provide detailed insights into the composition and purity of various substances.“

  • Over 10 years of experience with chemical analysis of PEDs and related compounds.
  • State-of-the-art laboratory equipment.
  • Globally recognized & trusted by thousands of clients.

Our Mission & Values

At Janoshik, our goal is simple – to provide scientifically accurate, independent, and transparent chemical analysis that ensures product safety and quality.
